1. Start GTN (1 mg/ml) at 1.5 ml/h
2. Increase rate by 1.0 ml/h every 5 min until target achieved
3. If target not reached after 30 min (typically 7.5 ml/h), consider continuing GTN at rate achieved and adding labetalol, if not already given.
4. If target is reached, continue infusion and check BP every 5 min for 15 min, then every 15 min for 1h, then hourly up to 24 h
5. If BP falls to < 130 mmHg, stop GTN infusion
6. Restart if SBP > target in first 24 h, initially at 1.5 ml/h
7. Titrate by 0.5-1.0 ml/h to keep SBP at 130-140 mmHg
